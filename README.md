# Leave Well Alone 🎮  
*A short point & click adventure game made in 7 days*

---

## 📖 How to Play
- 🗂 **Inventory** is located at the top of the screen.
- 🖱 **Left-click** → Walk, interact, skip dialog.
- 🖱 **Right-click** → Deselect inventory items.
